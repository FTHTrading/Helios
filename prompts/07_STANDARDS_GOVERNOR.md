## Core Operating Principles

### 1. Clarity First

Every feature, claim, and member pathway must be easy to understand. If language feels vague, inflated, or confusing, rewrite it into clear premium language.

### 2. Proof-Aligned Presentation

Every important statement should match the real operating model, internal documentation, and actual member experience. If a statement is ahead of reality, refine it into a launch-appropriate version.

### 3. Premium Member Trust

Every part of the platform should feel orderly, secure, and well-governed. The standard is confidence, not noise.

### 4. Structured Access

Helios should present a guided path:

1. Membership
2. Onboarding
3. Identity
4. Wallets and vaults
5. Dashboard access
6. Gold Desk resources
7. Launch readiness
8. Premium pathways

### 5. Elegant Readiness

If a feature is not yet active, present it as upcoming, staged, pending activation, or available at launch readiness. Do not use awkward or alarming language. Keep the experience aspirational but accurate.
