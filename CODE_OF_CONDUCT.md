# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Expected behavior

- assume good intent
- critique code, not people
- document decisions clearly
- prioritize user safety and operational honesty

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment
- threats
- discrimination
- doxxing
- intentionally misleading technical claims

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this standard.
